Who We Are

About Next Step

Bridging the Career Guidance Gap in Lango

In the Lango sub-region of Uganda, many students struggle to make informed decisions about their future careers and professional paths. Limited exposure to diverse career options, lack of guidance in schools, and insufficient counseling support frequently lead to poor academic performance, low motivation, and eventual unemployment.

Recognizing this critical gap, the Next Step Career Guidance and Counseling Services Initiative was established to bridge the divide — empowering students with the knowledge, skills, and professional guidance necessary to make informed career choices and take proactive steps toward their future professions.

We begin with government-aided schools in the Lango sub-region and are committed to expanding our reach to private schools and beyond.

Our Mission

"To empower students in the Lango sub-region with the knowledge, skills, and professional guidance necessary to make informed career choices and take proactive steps toward purposeful, fulfilling futures."

Informed Choice

We equip every student with the knowledge they need to choose careers aligned with their true interests, abilities, and the opportunities the market offers.

Partnership

We collaborate with schools, education authorities, professionals, NGOs, and government agencies to deliver impactful, well-resourced guidance.

Inclusivity

Every student — from rural villages to urban centers — deserves equal access to quality career guidance, regardless of background.

Impact

We monitor and evaluate our work rigorously to ensure measurable improvements in students' career decision-making and motivation.

Sustainability

We build diverse partnerships and community ownership so our work continues to grow and serve students long into the future.

Empowerment

We believe in the potential of every young person to succeed — our role is to ignite that potential with the right guidance and support.

Our Objectives

What We Set Out to Achieve

  • To provide professional career guidance and counseling to students in primary and secondary schools in the Lango sub-region.
  • To equip students with comprehensive knowledge about various professions, career paths, and educational opportunities.
  • To reduce career indecision and the mismatch between students' interests, abilities, and chosen career paths.
  • To build strategic partnerships with professionals and institutions to support students' career development.
