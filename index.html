About Us

Our Background

In the Lango sub-region of Uganda many students struggle to make informed decisions about their careers and professional paths. This results from limited exposure to diverse career options, lack of guidance in school and insufficient counselling support.

Our Mission

To empower students in the Lango sub-region with career guidance mentorship and counselling services that enable them to make informed academic and career decisions.

Our Vision

A generation of students in Northern Uganda who are empowered confident and prepared to pursue fulfilling careers.

Key Objectives

  • Provide professional career guidance
  • Equip students with knowledge about professions
  • Reduce career indecision
  • Build partnerships with institutions
