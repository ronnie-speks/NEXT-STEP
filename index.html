Bridging the Career Guidance Gap in Lango

In the Lango sub-region of Uganda, many students struggle to make informed decisions about their future careers — often choosing paths that don't align with their skills, interests, or market demands.

The Next Step Initiative exists to close that gap. We connect students with professional guidance, mentorship, and practical resources so that every young person can take their next step with confidence and clarity.
